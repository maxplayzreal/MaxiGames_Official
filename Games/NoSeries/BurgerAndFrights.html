Burger and Frights

Description

It's a late night and you were craving burgers. It's time to ride home. Burger & Frights is a short horror game in which the player rides a bike throughout a dark forest. The game was inspired by games such as Rides with Strangers, and a certain horror movie by John Carpenter.
