Papa's Burgeria

Description

Papa Louie is Back in Papa’s Burgeria! Papa's Burgeria is a cooking game of Papa game series in which you run the business of a burger restaurant. In this game, you can choose to play as either Marty or Rita. Like other cooking games, your task is to take the customer’s orders, prepare and decorate the burgers, then serve your customer a perfect dish. Try to make the best burger and get high praise from your guests! As you level up, new guests will visit your restaurant and give more complicated requirements. As a result, the number of guests will increase. In order to not be overwhelmed, you'll have to learn how to manage your time to get the highest praise from your customers.

Description by Papa's Pizzeria
