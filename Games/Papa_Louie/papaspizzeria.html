Papa's Pizzeria

Description

Papa's Pizzeria is a typical cooking and restaurant management game where you take on the role of Roy and try to serve the best pizzas to the customers, make them happy, and earn money during the time Papa Louie leaves town.

Description by Papa's Pizzeria
