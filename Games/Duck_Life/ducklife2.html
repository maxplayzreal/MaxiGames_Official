Duck Life 2

Description

Duck Life 2 is an adventure game where you train your duck to race in various disciplines such as running, flying, swimming and climbing to be the best duck adventurer in the world.

Description by Poki

Controls

Use mouse to interact with UI

Use Up and Down arrow keys to Jump and Dive. And Left and Right to move duck.

Additional Info

Game Engine: Adobe Flash | Emulated with Ruffle

Original Release Date: July 13, 2010
